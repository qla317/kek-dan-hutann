About Us
Kek dan Hutan

Welcome to Kek Dan Hutan. Kek & Hutan is a delightful spot located in Kota Bharu, Malaysia, known for its authentic Sarawak cuisine. The menu features a variety of dishes, including Laksa Sarawak and Mee Kolok, which are highly praised for their flavors. The cafe also offers a selection of layered cakes, particularly popular for their sweetness and texture. Overall, Kek & Hutan is a must-visit for anyone looking to experience traditional Malaysian flavors in a cozy atmosphere.
